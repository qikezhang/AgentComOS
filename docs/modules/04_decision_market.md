# Decision Market

Decision Market is the default option discovery mechanism for all tasks with uncertainty.

## Levels
- skip: only trivial deterministic tasks
- mini: 3 proposals, lightweight critic
- standard: 5-7 proposals, scoring, critic, synthesis, final judge
- full: high risk, requires risk audit/release/founder approval if needed

## Required artifacts
Task classification, decision need score, proposal cards, proposal scores, critic reports, synthesized plan, final decision, decision record.
