# 11 Enterprise Adoption Guide

## Recommended pilot

Start with a low-risk operational workflow:

- service health check
- log retrieval
- controlled restart of non-critical service
- bounded AI work loop
- Manual OS audit

## Employee training

Train employees to:

- write clear operational requests
- understand blocked vs completed
- review evidence packets
- approve high-risk operations
- read GM reports
- respond to token/security incidents
- understand backup and retention boundaries
